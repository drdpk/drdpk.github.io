My research projects

Here is a list of my current and past research projects related to DNA data storage. You can click on the links to access the project websites or reports.

  • DNA Data Storage and Retrieval using Nanopore Sequencing: This is my current project at NUS, funded by the NRF. The goal of this project is to develop a novel method for retrieving data from DNA using nanopore sequencing, which is a fast and portable technology that can read single molecules of DNA. The project involves designing and testing new encoding and decoding algorithms, as well as optimizing the synthesis and sequencing processes.
