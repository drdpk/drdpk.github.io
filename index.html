About

Hello! I’m Dr. Deepak Sharma (PhD), and I’m currently working at the School of Computing, National University of Singapore. My research focuses on DNA computing and DNA-based data storage. I’m passionate about exploring new techniques and technologies that will shape the future of data storage. I believe that DNA digital data storage has enormous potential because of its high storage density, and I’m excited to be at the forefront of developing new methods to make it more practical and accessible. Thank you for visiting my website, and I hope you find my work interesting! If you have any questions or comments, please don’t hesitate to contact me.
