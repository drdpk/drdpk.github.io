Welcome to my website

I am a biotechnologist and I am working on DNA based data storage. I have done PhD in this field and I have published a few research journals and book chapters. I have also presented my work in some international conferences. Currently I am working at NUS as a postdoctoral researcher.

My research interests include DNA synthesis, sequencing, encoding, decoding, error correction, and compression. I am fascinated by the potential of DNA as a universal and durable medium for storing digital information. I am also interested in the applications of DNA data storage in biotechnology, medicine, and security.

Here you can find more information about me, my publications, my research projects, and how to contact me. I hope you enjoy browsing my website and learning more about my work.
